# Safety Guidelines

**Purpose:** Content policies, ethical boundaries, and safety constraints governing AI behavior and outputs.

**What Belongs Here:**
- Content policy and acceptable use guidelines
- Prohibited content types and topics
- Ethical guidelines and principles
- Bias detection and mitigation strategies
- Privacy and data protection guidelines
- Harmful content filtering rules
- Escalation procedures for edge cases
- Audit and monitoring guidelines

**What Does NOT Belong Here:**
- System prompts (see ../system_prompts/)
- Response patterns (see ../response_patterns/)
- Model configurations (see ../model_configs/)
- Compliance frameworks or regulations

**Expected Structure:**
Policy and guideline documentation files covering safety dimensions.
