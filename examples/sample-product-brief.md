# Sample Product Brief

## Product Name
Simple Habit Tracker

## Problem Statement
Busy professionals struggle to maintain consistent habits because they do not have a simple system that fits into their daily routine.

## Target User
Professionals who want to build small daily habits without excessive setup or complexity.

## Desired Outcome
Help the user create and maintain one or two habits with minimal friction.

## MVP Scope
### Must Have
- create a habit
- mark a habit as completed
- view daily progress

### Should Have
- simple reminders
- streak tracking

### Won't Have in MVP
- social features
- complex analytics
- integrations with third-party tools

## Success Metric
The user completes at least one habit on 5 of 7 days in the first week.
